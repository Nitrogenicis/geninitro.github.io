netcavy.net homepage

Netcavy

Shinkonet Cavy is an Australian based media generalist, focusing on various genres of music. His website uses a colour palette consistent with his brand. It shows its media in a compact style, which is further put in to the spotlight when view in an aspect ratio of a handheld device.

Best Aspects

Media is plentiful. Most of the pages contains media that links the user to other sites. This is further emphasised when viewed on handheld devices. It hides various aspects of the site and rearranged the footer to be part of a dropdown menu at the top of the screen.

Consistent style. A majority of his works have been music produced in a eurobeat, techno, or synthesised genre. This reflects well with his choice of typography and colour. There typography of the headings are very angular and sharp, which mimics a synthesised sound wave.
